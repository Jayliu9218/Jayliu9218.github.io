Coordinatie Convertion from $xyz$ to $r\theta z$

Created2023-03-10|Updated2024-09-06
|Post Views:

简介

以传热方程为基础，推导直角坐标、柱坐标与球坐标三者的转换关系

计算过程

直角坐标系下的热流量

$$
\Phi _x =-\lambda \frac{\partial t}{\partial x}dydz
$$

$$
\Phi {x+dx} = -\lambda \frac{\partial t}{\partial x}dydz +\frac{\partial \Phi{x} }{\partial x}dx=-\lambda \frac{\partial t}{\partial x}dydz +[-\frac{\partial (-\lambda \frac{\partial t}{\partial x}dydz)}{\partial x}]dx
$$

$$
\Phi _{x}-\Phi _{x+dx}= \frac{\partial (\lambda \frac{\partial t}{\partial x}dydz)}{\partial x}
$$
同理可得$y,z$方向的热流量

直角坐标系下的传热微分方程

$$
\frac{\partial (\lambda \frac{\partial t}{\partial x}dydz)}{\partial x}dx+\frac{\partial (\lambda \frac{\partial t}{\partial y}dxdz)}{\partial y}dy+\frac{\partial (\lambda \frac{\partial t}{\partial z}dxdy)}{\partial z}dz+\dot{\Phi}dxdydz=\rho c \frac{\partial t}{\partial \tau}dxdydz
$$

转换规则

规定坐标转化必须在同一量度之下，不属于同一量度的需先补齐，补齐部分仅作为系数

  1. 柱坐标系下
    $$
    r\to x,dr\to dx
    \
    r\phi \to y, d(r\phi)=rd\phi\to dy
    \
    z\to z,dz\to dz
    $$
  2. 球坐标系下
    $$
    r\to x,dr\to dx
    \
    rsin\theta \phi \to y, d(rsin\theta \phi)=rsin\theta d\phi\to dy
    \
    r\theta \to z,d(r\theta)=rd\theta \to dz
    $$

转换过程

将转换规则中的结果代入直角坐标系中

  1. 柱坐标系下
    $$
    \frac{\partial (\lambda \frac{\partial t}{\partial r}rd\phi dz)}{\partial r}dr+\frac{\partial (\lambda \frac{\partial t}{r\partial \phi}drdz)}{r\partial \phi}rd\phi+\frac{\partial (\lambda \frac{\partial t}{\partial z}rdrd\phi)}{\partial z}dz+\dot{\Phi}rdrd\phi dz=\rho c \frac{\partial t}{\partial \tau}rdrd\phi dz
    $$
    考虑到坐标系的正交性，可以化简得到
    $$
    \frac{1}{r}\frac{\partial (\lambda r\frac{\partial t}{\partial r})}{\partial r}+\frac{1}{r^2}\frac{\partial (\lambda \frac{\partial t}{\partial \phi})}{\partial \phi}+\frac{\partial (\lambda \frac{\partial t}{\partial z})}{\partial z}+\dot{\Phi}=\rho c \frac{\partial t}{\partial \tau}
    $$
  2. 球坐标系下
    $$
    \frac{\partial (\lambda \frac{\partial t}{\partial r}r^2sin\theta d\phi d\theta )}{\partial r}dr+\frac{\partial (\lambda \frac{\partial t}{rsin\theta \partial \phi}rdrd\theta)}{rsin\theta \partial \phi}rsin\theta d\phi+\frac{\partial (\lambda \frac{\partial t}{r\partial \theta}rsin\theta dr d\phi)}{r\partial \theta}rd\theta+\dot{\Phi}r^2 sin\theta drd\phi d\theta =\rho c \frac{\partial t}{\partial \tau}r^2 sin\theta drd\phi d\theta
    $$
    考虑到坐标系的正交性，可以化简得到
    $$
    \frac{1}{r^2}\frac{\partial (\lambda r^2\frac{\partial t}{\partial r})}{\partial r}+\frac{1}{r^2 sin^2\theta }\frac{\partial (\lambda \frac{\partial t}{\partial \phi})}{\partial \phi}+\frac{1}{r^2sin\theta}\frac{\partial (\lambda sin\theta \frac{\partial t}{\partial \theta})}{\partial \theta}+\dot{\Phi}=\rho c \frac{\partial t}{\partial \tau}
    $$

拉普拉斯算子

设$\lambda$各向同性,，在直角坐标系下
$$
\frac{\partial ^2 t}{\partial x^2}+\frac{\partial ^2 t}{\partial y^2}+\frac{\partial ^2 t}{\partial z^2}+\frac{\dot{\Phi} }{\lambda}=\frac{\rho c}{\lambda} \frac{\partial t}{\partial \tau}
$$

$$
\nabla ^2 t+\frac{\dot{\Phi} }{\lambda}=\frac{\rho c}{\lambda} \frac{\partial t}{\partial \tau}
$$
考虑到上式$=$号恒成立
柱坐标系下
$$
\nabla ^2 t=\frac{1}{r}\frac{\partial (r\frac{\partial t}{\partial r})}{\partial r}+\frac{1}{r^2}\frac{\partial (\frac{\partial t}{\partial \phi})}{\partial \phi}+\frac{\partial (\frac{\partial t}{\partial z})}{\partial z}=\frac{\partial ^2t}{ {\partial r^2} }+\frac{1}{r}\frac{\partial t}{ {\partial r} }+\frac{1}{r^2}\frac{\partial ^2t}{\partial \phi ^2}+\frac{\partial ^2}{\partial z^2}
$$
球坐标系下
$$
\nabla ^2 t=\frac{1}{r^2}\frac{\partial (r^2\frac{\partial t}{\partial r})}{\partial r}+\frac{1}{r^2 sin^2\theta }\frac{\partial (\frac{\partial t}{\partial \phi})}{\partial \phi}+\frac{1}{r^2sin\theta}\frac{\partial (sin\theta \frac{\partial t}{\partial \theta})}{\partial \theta}=\frac{\partial ^2 t}{\partial r^2}+\frac{2}{r}\frac{\partial t}{\partial r}+\frac{1}{r^2sin^2 \theta}\frac{\partial ^2t}{\partial \phi ^2}+\frac{1}{r^2
}\frac{\partial ^2t}{\partial \theta ^2}+\frac{cos\theta}{r^2 sin\theta}\frac{\partial t}{\partial \theta}
$$
