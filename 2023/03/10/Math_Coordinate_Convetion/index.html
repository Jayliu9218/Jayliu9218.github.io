Coordinatie Convertion from $xyz$ to $r\theta z$

Created2023-03-10|Updated2023-03-10
|Post View:

简介

直角坐标系下的热流量

$$
\Phi _x =-\lambda \frac{\partial t}{\partial x}dydz
$$

$$
\Phi {x+dx} = -\lambda \frac{\partial t}{\partial x}dydz +\frac{\partial \Phi{x}}{\partial x}dx=-\lambda \frac{\partial t}{\partial x}dydz +[-\frac{\partial (-\lambda \frac{\partial t}{\partial x}dydz)}{\partial x}]dx
$$
同理可得$y,z$方向的热流量

直角坐标系下的传热微分方程

$$
\frac{\partial (\lambda \frac{\partial t}{\partial x}dydz)}{\partial x}dx+\frac{\partial (\lambda \frac{\partial t}{\partial y}dxdz)}{\partial y}dy+\frac{\partial (\lambda \frac{\partial t}{\partial z}dxdy)}{\partial z}dz+\dot{\Phi}dxdydz=\rho c \frac{\partial t}{\partial \tau}dxdydz
$$

转换规则

规定坐标转化必须在同一量度之下，不属于同一量度的需先补齐，补齐部分仅作为系数

  1. 柱坐标系下
    $$
    r\to x,dr\to dx,\partial r\to \partial x
    \
    r\phi \to y, d(r\phi)=rd\phi\to dy, \partial (r\phi)=r\partial \phi \to \partial y
    \
    z\to z,dz\to dz,\partial z\to \partial z
    $$

转换过程

将转换规则中的结果代入直角坐标系中
$$
\frac{\partial (\lambda \frac{\partial t}{\partial r}rd\phi dz)}{\partial r}dr+\frac{\partial (\lambda \frac{\partial t}{r\partial \phi}drdz)}{r\partial \phi}rd\phi+\frac{\partial (\lambda \frac{\partial t}{\partial z}dxdy)}{\partial z}dz+\dot{\Phi}rdrd\phi dz=\rho c \frac{\partial t}{\partial \tau}rdrd\phi dz
$$
考虑到坐标系的正交性
$$
\frac{\partial(d\phi)}{dr}=0
$$
可以化简得到
$$
\frac{1}{r}\frac{\partial (\lambda r\frac{\partial t}{\partial r})}{\partial r}dr+\frac{1}{r^2}\frac{\partial (\lambda \frac{\partial t}{\partial \phi})}{\partial \phi}+\frac{\partial (\lambda \frac{\partial t}{\partial z})}{\partial z}+\dot{\Phi}=\rho c \frac{\partial t}{\partial \tau}
$$
